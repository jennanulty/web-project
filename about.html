about

Hi there, I’m Taylor Gatlin.

I grew up in a town called Bakersfield in the Central Valley of California and journeyed over to San Luis Obispo when I started my college career in 2013. I’m the oldest of six siblings, I know it’s cray cray! But I absolutely love my family and friends! I’m currently majoring in Agribusiness at Cal Poly while my passion is coordinating events.

I started getting an itch for planning while volunteering for a nonprofit called Young Life. Something about bringing people together on a weekly basis to make memories was so beautiful and precious to me. I later interned for Big Brothers Big Sisters in San Luis Obispo and helped them plan one of their major fundraisers! I loved the adrenaline rush on the day of and seeing everything we had been planning for months in action. I couldn’t get enough of the coordination side of things so I decided to reach out to an event coordination and design company. (I actually emailed over 100 people in the industry asking for an internship/part-time job/literally anything and only got one hopeful reply.)

Little did I know that this internship was going to introduce me to my dream job, the most loving and grace giving bosses & coworkers, PLUS allow me to explore all things weddings. Guys, it’s seriously the best thing ever when you get to live out your dream! I started working for Zest It Up in the summer of 2016 and am currently still there. This EVENTure (I’m a sucker for puns) is just the start to something big! I love to help people make memories and bring together their family and friends. I can’t think of a better way then to help them plan their wedding day and make their dreams come true as well!

Cheers, Taylor
